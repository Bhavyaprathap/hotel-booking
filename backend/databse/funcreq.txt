1. Abstract

The Hospital Management System (HMS) is a software solution designed to digitalize and automate all major day-to-day operations of a hospital. This includes patient registration, appointment scheduling, doctor consultation, electronic medical records, billing, pharmacy management, laboratory integration, and inpatient services. The system aims to reduce manual workload, minimize human errors, increase efficiency, and provide secure access to patient health information. HMS improves communication between departments and enables seamless coordination among doctors, nurses, pharmacists, and administrative staff.

2. Functional Requirements (FR)
FR1: Patient Registration

Register new patients with personal, medical, and contact details.

Assign unique Patient ID.

FR2: Appointment Management

Book, modify, or cancel doctor appointments.

Auto-allocate slots based on doctor availability.

FR3: Electronic Medical Records (EMR)

Maintain each patientâ€™s past medical history.

Store prescriptions, diagnoses, test results.

FR4: Doctor Module

Doctors can view appointments, update prescriptions, and access medical history.

FR5: Billing & Payments

Auto-generate bills for consultation, tests, pharmacy, and admission.

Support UPI, card, cash.

FR6: Pharmacy Module

Manage medicine inventory.

Check drug availability and expiry dates.

FR7: Laboratory Management

Manage lab test requests.

Upload and store lab results.

FR8: Inpatient Management

Bed allotment, nurse assignment, treatment tracking.

FR9: Admin Module

Access control, staff creation, department management.

3. Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)
NFR1: Security

Patient records must be encrypted.

Role-based access (Doctor, Nurse, Admin).

NFR2: Reliability

Backup every 24 hours.

99.9% uptime.

NFR3: Performance

System should load patient data within 3 seconds.

NFR4: Scalability

Support multi-branch hospitals in the future.

NFR5: Usability

User-friendly UI for medical staff with minimal training.

NFR6: Availability

System must run 24x7, even during emergencies.

1. Abstract

The Banking Management System is designed to automate and secure core banking services like account creation, fund transfers, transaction history, loan management, and customer support. It ensures secure communication, real-time processing of transactions, and accurate financial records. The system reduces manual paper-based processes and enhances customer satisfaction through instant digital services.

2. Functional Requirements (FR)
FR1: User Authentication

Login via username/password and OTP.

FR2: Account Management

Create, update, or delete bank accounts.

View account balance.

FR3: Fund Transfer

IMPS, NEFT, RTGS within or outside the bank.

FR4: Transaction History

Display list of past transactions.

FR5: Loan Management

Apply for loans.

Admin approves or rejects.

FR6: Bill and Utility Payments

Electricity, water, phone bills.

FR7: Customer Support

Inquiry and complaint system.

FR8: Admin Module

Manage employee accounts.

Monitor transactions.

3. Non-Functional Requirements (NFR)
NFR1: Security

AES-256 encryption

Two-factor authentication

Anti-fraud monitoring

NFR2: Performance

Fund transfers must be processed in <5 seconds.

NFR3: Availability

99.99% uptime.

NFR4: Reliability

All transactions stored with audit trail.

NFR5: Backup

Automatic backup multiple times per day.
